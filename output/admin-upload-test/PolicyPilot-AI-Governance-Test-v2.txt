AI Governance and Responsible Use Standard
Version: 2.0
Effective Date: 2026-02-19
Owner: Security and GRC

1. Purpose
This policy defines mandatory controls for safe, compliant, and auditable use of AI systems.

2. Scope    
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and systems that use AI for business operations.

3. Role-Based Obligations

3.1 Executive Obligations (Role Track: exec)
- Approve high-risk AI use cases before production launch.
- Ensure each business unit maintains an AI risk register reviewed monthly.
- Require documented human oversight for customer-impacting AI decisions.
- Escalate unresolved AI compliance risks to Security and Legal within 24 hours.

3.2 Builder Obligations (Role Track: builder)
- Do not use unapproved datasets for model training, tuning, or prompt context.
- Log model and prompt changes in the engineering change record before deployment.
- Implement output validation and safety checks for all user-facing AI responses.
- Block production deployment when policy checks or governance gates fail.
- Redact secrets and personal data from prompts unless an approved exception exists.

3.3 General Workforce Obligations (Role Track: general)
- Use only approved AI tools for business tasks.
- Never paste confidential, regulated, or customer-sensitive data into non-approved tools.
- Verify AI-generated output before sharing externally or using it for decisions.
- Report suspicious, harmful, or policy-violating AI behavior to Security immediately.

4. Mandatory Control Requirements
- Control AI-01: Approved Use Boundaries
  All AI use must align to approved use cases and data boundaries.
- Control AI-02: Human Oversight
  Customer-impacting outputs require documented human review and sign-off.
- Control AI-03: Adoption Evidence
  Completion, acknowledgment, quiz, and attestation records must be retained for audit.

5. Enforcement
Violations may result in access restriction, corrective actions, and formal escalation.

6. Audit and Review
This policy is reviewed quarterly or after significant regulatory or business changes.
