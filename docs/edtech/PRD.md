# PRD: PolicyPilot MVP

## Goal

Ship a pilot-ready enterprise product for AI compliance learning in 6 weeks.

## ICP

- SaaS companies with 200-2000 employees
- Stakeholders: L&D, Security, Legal, and operational managers

## MVP scope

- Policy ingestion and obligation extraction
- Role-track module generation (`exec`, `builder`, `general`)
- Assignment management and completion tracking
- Quiz attempts and pass/fail scoring
- Attestation capture with integrity checksum
- CSV and PDF audit exports
- Reminder nudges

## Success criteria

- First campaign published in under 45 minutes
- 75%+ completion in pilot cohort
- 90%+ attestation among completers
- Export generation under 30 seconds
